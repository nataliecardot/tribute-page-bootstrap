A brief timeline in events of David Hume's life:


  • 1711 – Born as David Home in Edinburgh, Scotland
  • 1713 – Father dies
  • 1723 – Enrolls at University of Edinburgh at the age of 12 (14 was typical)
  • 1734 – Changes surname to Hume
  • 1739 – Publishes Books 1 and 2 of A Treatise on Human Nature
  • 1748 – Publishes An Enquiry Concerning Human Understanding
  • 1751 – Publishes An Enquiry Concerning the Principles of Morals
  • 1776 – Dies at the age of 65
